Bangladesh Unemployment Rate Dashboard

Key Findings

  • Youth unemployment (15-24) remains 2-4 times higher than adult rates
  • Male youth unemployment peaked at 14.92% in 2022 before dropping to 11.52% in 2023
  • Female youth unemployment decreased dramatically from 16.80% (2017) to 4.72% (2023)
  • Overall improvement since 2017 across all age groups and genders
  • Gender disparity reversed - while male rates were lower historically, since 2020 female rates have been significantly lower
